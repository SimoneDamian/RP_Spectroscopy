Depending on the bool state of the enabled entry of the fast_control_signal_derivative and slow_control_signal_derivative (that the user can change from the @advanced_settings_page.py), the LockingMonitorPlotHandler in @plot_panel.py has to show, in the same plot but with a separate y-axis, also the derivative of the associated signal, that is already contained in the packet that is sent to the GUI from the laser manager (look at get_history method in the @interface.py).
